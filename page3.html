These first three places are, of course, only a small sampling of all the ones I wish to visit. On this page, I've included a few more places that are high on my list but didn't make the cut to get an entire page about them. Many of these places are also so high up on my list simply because, due to rapidly changing social, political, and environmental factors, they may not be what they are today in only a couple decades. Nonetheless, they're all places that seem super interesting to me, and I very much still want to visit each and every one of them as soon as I'm able.

Regardless of whether or not you feel similarly about any of the places on my particular list, I encourage you to think yourself about those places that you want to visit, for whatever reason, even if it may be out of reach at the moment. Dreams are what keep us going as people, and having such a clear one as getting to a physical place can be an especially potent one! Do your best to use these dreams simply as goals, though, not as an escape from your own life. Do feel strongly about these dreams, but not so strongly that they overtake who you are and what you are doing right now. Today, do what you are able to, and tomorrow, you might just be a step or two closer to reaching that place of yours!.
